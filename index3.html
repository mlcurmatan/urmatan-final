Academic Biography

Larry Urmatan is a Computer Engineering student at the Technological Institute of the Philippines (Manila) and a former student of Mapúa University (Intramuros). He specializes in the field of mechatronics with robotics, and railway engineering. For over a decade, his primary research focus has been the development of sensors, feedback systems, and electronic instrumentation designed to evaluate atmospheric and weather phenomena. Beyond his technical pursuits, Larry is a dedicated student leader and educator, having served as a District Information and Liaison Committee Chairperson and as a facilitator for various technical seminars and collaborative research initiatives.
